Our Mission

AlgoZenith Campus Connection Program is an Agozenith initiative to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands. We empower students with resources, mentorship, and a platform to lead change on their campuses, and create a meaningful impact among the student community.

Application Process

  1. Application Review We review your application to assess leadership potential and program fit.
  2. Phone Interaction Shortlisted candidates get a quick call to discuss motivation and interest.
  3. 1:1 Video Interview & Onboarding Finalists attend a 20–25 min interview, and selected leads are onboarded into AZ CCP.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the AZ Campus Connection Program?

AZ CCP is a student leadership initiative by AlgoZenith that empowers students to establish and lead campus chapters, organize a wide variety of events, and foster a collaborative learning community with full institutional support.

Who is eligible to apply?

Applicants must be full-time UG/PG students enrolled in a recognized institution in India (pursuing BTech, MTech, BCA, or MCA, including integrated programs). First-year and final-year students are NOT eligible.

Is this a paid position?

o, it is a voluntary leadership role. However, it offers significant non-monetary benefits such as exclusive perks, professional networking, and career opportunities.

What is the time commitment?

The commitment is flexible but driven by your activity. This is a functional role, and your weekly hours will vary based on where you are in the event planning cycle. This typically involves 2-3 hours per week for planning, promotion, team coordination, and event execution, ensuring a continuous impact on your campus community.

What kind of events do chapters organize?

ur chapters host diverse events to provide students with a complete placement readiness experience. This covers workshops and seminars for skill development, coding contests, web development and sessions on crucial career aspects like resume building, interview preparation, and overall career guidance.

Do I need to be a technical student?

Eligibility is open to students in BTech, MTech, BCA, or MCA programs. We welcome students from any branch/domain within these degrees, as the key is your ability to drive technical community initiatives.
